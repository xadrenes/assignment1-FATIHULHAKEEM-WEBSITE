CONTRIBUTION TO PERLIS

As the owner of Kampung Wai Cafe, Muhammad Fatihul Hakeem contributes directly to the local economy of Perlis by providing food and beverage services to the community and visitors.

Through his business, he helps promote local culinary culture and creates opportunities for local employment. He also uses digital marketing strategies to increase the visibility of Kampung Wai Cafe, attracting both local and outside customers.

His contribution supports the growth of small business development in Perlis and helps strengthen the local tourism and food industry.
